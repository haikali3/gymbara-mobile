# Gymbara Mobile Beginner Workout Plan

## Overview
This plan is designed for complete beginners who have never been to the gym before. It features fixed, simple exercises to build strength, confidence, and fitness gradually. The app targets a 3-Day Split routine, training 3 days per week with rest days in between for recovery.

## Target Audience
- Beginners new to gym workouts
- Focus on safe, effective compound movements
- Goal: Build muscle, improve strength, and establish healthy habits

## Weekly Schedule
- **Monday**: Day 1 - Upper Body
- **Wednesday**: Day 2 - Lower Body
- **Friday**: Day 3 - Full Body
- Rest on other days; include light walking or stretching if desired.

Alternate the starting day each week to balance.

## Progression Tips
- Start with light weights or bodyweight to learn proper form.
- Increase weight when you can complete all sets and reps comfortably.
- Rest 1-2 minutes between sets.
- Warm up with 5-10 minutes of light cardio.
- Cool down with stretching.

## Nutrition Advice
- Eat a balanced diet with plenty of protein (chicken, eggs, etc.), carbs (rice, oats), and healthy fats.
- Aim for a calorie surplus if building muscle, or deficit if losing fat.
- Stay hydrated and get enough sleep.
